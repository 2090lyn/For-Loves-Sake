# GAMES

## For Love's Sake

### Objective
Cathlyn was captured and locked away by bokbok. Save her by collecting **3 hearts** to prove your love.

---

## Heart Challenges

### 1. Scavenger Hunt for Notes ❤️
A book lost its pages and will give you a heart in exchange for finding them.

Tasks include:
- Forest riddle to receive a note
- Fishing minigame
  - Fish can help you regenerate a heart
- Other notes hidden throughout:
  - 2D world
  - House

---

### 2. Ping Pong Game ❤️
- Located inside the house
- Player must first find a hidden switch to unlock the game

---

### 3. 2D Platform Parkour ❤️
- Complete the platforming challenge to earn a heart

---

## Final Challenges

### Battle with Chicken
- Pokémon-style battle
- Defeat the chicken to obtain a key

### Dungeon Maze
- Use the key to enter the dungeon and rescue Cathlyn
- Maze contains:
  - Enemies
  - 3 lives total
- Losing a life restarts the maze
